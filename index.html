About

Through my work I determine and create the visual language to communicate a message, story, and point of view.

My name is Sahil Babbar. Armed with a BA in Communications from Central Saint Martins, University of the Arts London, I work across art direction, photography, and creative direction — spanning fashion, luxury beauty, and editorial.

My practice spans the full arc of creative production: from brand identity and campaign concepting to on-set direction and final delivery across every touchpoint. I build visual systems that are both rigorous in concept and precise in execution.

Featured in the British Journal of Photography 'Ones to Watch' and Wallpaper Magazine 'NEW GEN'.
